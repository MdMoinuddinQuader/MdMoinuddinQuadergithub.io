My Experience & Achievement from Digi Fab Program in AKGEC

Md. Moinuddin Quader

 

First of all I would like to give big thanks the AKGEC family to invite us. I am very pleased with your reception.

 

I am Md. Moinuddin Quader. I have completed B.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering from Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology previous year. After graduation I joined the project “Establishment of Fab Lab in CUET” as Fab Guru. But I am just an apprentice in the field of Fab Lab. So, I am very much interested about this “DigiFab Program”. Hopefully it will satisfy my expectation about breaking my limitation in product prototyping.

Lunch Break: AKGEC provided very traditional & tasty food for us in a calm environment.

 

Session 2: This session was campus tour. We visited the whole campus which is very neat & clean. They have very well equipped lab which are very efficient also to my view.

 

 

DAY 02

Session 1: In this session, we have learnt about 3D Printer. We discussed basic function of 3D Printer. Additive manufacturing process is followed in 3D printing. They also gave us idea about 3D printer manufacturing. Basiclly 3D printer used for prototyping as it is too much time consuming. We also explore some software such as cura, grabCAD , makerbot etc. Every software support .STL format. There is some important parameter in this software. They are:

1)      In fill density

2)      Time

3)      Surface roughness

4)      Material

5)      Layer Thickness

6)      orientation  

Layer thickness is reversely proportional to the time & surface finish.

Then we took some model which were printed in makerbot.
